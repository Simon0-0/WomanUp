Podcast

Tea, wine, tequila is a podcast of the WomanUp project, in which our founder Nat Pavlovská confesses inspiring women from a completely different angle. We divided the questions into 3 levels - we start lightly with tea, then gradually move on to deeper topics with a glass of wine in hand, until we end up with secrets and taboos ...
